S'mores Sundaes





Description

S’mores Sundaes are the best kind of s’mores for a hot summer day. These sundaes are s’mores ice cream perfection.
A S’mores Sundae is the perfect way to enjoy s’mores on a hot summer day. Vanilla ice cream, graham cracker crumbs, toasted marshmallows, and hot fudge make this one of the easiest s’mores treats to make.
National S’mores Day, one of the best food holidays is definitely a day to be celebrated. I just can’t wait until August to do that so it is s’more recipes in just about any form all year long for me.

Ingredients

  • 1 pint vanilla or chocolate ice cream
  • ½ cup crushed graham crackers
  • ¼ cup chocolate chips
  • 1 cup miniature marshmallows
  • ½ cup hot fudge

Instructions

  1. Spread the marshmallows into a single layer on a parchment paper lined baking sheet. Turn oven broiler to high and broil the marshmallows for 30 seconds to 1 minute until just toasted.
  2. Place a scoop of ice cream in the bottom of each sundae glass. (I love how layered sundaes look in glass sundae dishes like these!) Sprinkle on a layer of chocolate chips, graham cracker crumbs, hot fudge, and toasted marshmallows. Add a second scoop of ice cream and a final layer of toppings.
